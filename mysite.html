Developer Profile

Great to have you here!
I'm Maxwell Mugendi from Kenya, pursuing APT at USIU-A. Being a developer is both challenging and fun; On one side you get to be creative to design but also logical to make functional systems.

I draw my inspiration from many sources, but especially entrepreneurs who changed the world by thinking outside the box to improve it.
To relax I like hiking, motorsports, skating, badminton and drone photography, or just sleeping.

I also have two pet parrots. They are African (fisher's) lovebirds found naturally around East Africa and Madagascar.
