Design Summary

IN-VR Sitemap

Description

The site sets to promote a Virtual Reality Exhibition, where different VR companies come to showcase what they have. The site is built with the Web structure, that allows linking to any page on the site from any page.

Functions

  1. The site allows Visitors to check out descriptions of booths, watch a video about the booth.
  2. Visitors can also check the schedule of the event and book tickets.
  3. Visitors can also sign the guestbook and leave feedback on the event
